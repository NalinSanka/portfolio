About Me

With more than 10 years of experience in mechanical design, 3D CAD development, product design, and LEGO model creation, I focus on building solutions that are both functional and visually refined. My work is driven by precision, structure, and a strong understanding of how good design performs in real use.

I specialize in SolidWorks-based modeling, assemblies, technical drawings, product development, mechanism design, and BrickLink Studio model creation. I enjoy transforming ideas into clear, buildable outcomes, whether for engineering systems, educational concepts, or creative model-based projects.

I work with a practical and detail-oriented mindset, balancing design quality, manufacturability, communication, and problem solving throughout the development process. My goal is always to create work that feels thoughtful, professional, and well resolved.
